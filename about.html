About Us

A legacy of excellence in luxury real estate, built on trust, expertise, and a passion for exceptional homes.

Our Story

Building Dreams
Since 2010

HavenHomes was founded with a simple mission: to connect discerning buyers with exceptional properties. What started as a boutique agency in Manhattan has grown into one of the most respected luxury real estate firms in the country.

Our success is built on deep market knowledge, personal relationships, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' vision. Every member of our team shares a passion for architecture, design, and the art of finding the perfect home.

Over 15 years later, we've helped hundreds of families find their dream homes, facilitated over $2 billion in transactions, and earned the trust of clients across the globe.
