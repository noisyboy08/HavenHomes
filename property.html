Description

Welcome to this stunning modern lakefront villa that redefines luxury living. Nestled along the pristine shores of Beverly Hills, this architectural masterpiece offers breathtaking panoramic water views from virtually every room.

The open-concept living space features soaring 20-foot ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows, and premium Italian marble flooring. The gourmet kitchen is a chef's dream, equipped with top-of-the-line Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances, a waterfall quartz island, and a walk-in pantry.

The master suite occupies the entire upper level, featuring a private balcony, spa-inspired ensuite bathroom with a freestanding soaking tub, rain shower, and dual vanities. Four additional bedrooms each with ensuite bathrooms provide ample space for family and guests.
